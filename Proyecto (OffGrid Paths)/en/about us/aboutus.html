About Us

Who we are

We are Francisco "Kiko", Rubén, Héctor and Pablo. Four lifelong friends who share the same passion: the mountains. Vista el Senderista, our faithful canine companion, is always by our side. What began as a way to unwind has become a way of life.

What we do

We travel mountain trails and document them from real experience. We upload routes we've done ourselves, along with everything they entail: crumpled maps, unique landscapes, and unforgettable anecdotes.

Why we do it

Because in the mountains, time has a different rhythm. Friendships are forged there, limits are challenged, and stories are found that aren't seen on social media. Hiking together transforms us, and we want to share that experience.

Vista el Senderista

Our dog, small and somewhat fearful, but more determined than many. Vista is an essential part of the pack. He never falls behind, and his instinct and energy have earned him respect on every journey.

Our mission

To inspire others to get outside, to reconnect with nature, to walk respectfully, and to enjoy things without rushing. We don't sell perfect adventures, but real experiences, with mud, effort, and a great reward: feeling like you're exactly where you want to be.
